Ember

Ember is the burned out remains of a war torn city. The fighting has moved on, but the conflicts remain. The people of Ember are tasked to rebuild and repair: not only their city, but their families, and themselves. Tonight marks the fifth anniversary of when the armies marched through the streets of Ember. Tonight you keep a tradition and light the candles to the lives lost.

Ember is an immersive two-day experience for 40 participants. The experience is “mechanic lite” and “story heavy”.
