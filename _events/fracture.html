Fracture

Fracture is a Nordic larp where a trip to a summer camp becomes a crucible for death and rebirth at the hands of mysterious cultists. Participants experience a weekend of splatterpunk and psychological horror that ends in a cosmic horror rebirth, becoming cultists themselves. Set in the Eskhaton universe with focus on the Extant cult, Fracture explores themes of breaking cycles and creating new meaning from rebirth.

Fracture ran August 30th - September 1st 2024 and included 80 participants.
